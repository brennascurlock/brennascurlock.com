Impromptu Text Designs

Created Spring 2010

Perhaps one of my favorite design assignments was the series of text "images" I created as part of a document design course.

Given a series of letters, our job was to create a recognizable image out of the type. Shown here are two images created with the letters D, W and T, and one with the word "surprising."

Image 1: sink with faucet and drop of water. Image 2: a "surprising" exclamation mark. Image 3: a quirky cartoon face.
