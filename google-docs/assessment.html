How well can you use Google docs?

Let's see if this tutorial was effective. I have invited you to edit my cover letter; however, I forgot to convert the Word file to a Google doc when I uploaded it. You will need to convert it to a Google doc, format the letter, and email it to me as a PDF. Good luck!

You can access my cover letter here. Here are some steps to get started:

  • You will need to download the Word file and upload it to your documents as a Google doc.
  • Format the text in Google docs. I removed all the formatting for you, so you can fix line breaks and spacing issues. The letter should follow a standard letter format.
  • Once you have the document edited and saved in your Google docs, email it as a PDF. (For the purpose of this exercise, you can email it to yourself.)
