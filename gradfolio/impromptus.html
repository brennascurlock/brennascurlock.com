Impromptu Text Designs

Created Spring 2010

Perhaps one of my favorite design assignments was the series of text "images" I created as part of a document design course.

Given a series of letters, our job was to create a recognizable image out of the type. Shown here are two images created with the letters D, W and T, and one with the word "surprising." Click the image to the right to view all three. If you have JavaScript disabled, you can follow the links below, instead.
